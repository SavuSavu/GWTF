What GWTF does

A specialized platform for spiral vase-mode printing with advanced toolpath control.

Seamless Helix

A single toolpath rises gradually — no stopping, no seams, no artifacts.

Wave Patterns

Blend sine, square, and triangle waves with interference for organic textures.

Blob Mode

Injection-molding style blobs for unique textured surfaces and dot patterns.

3D Preview

Interactive viewport with playback simulation, layer scrubbing, and speed controls.
